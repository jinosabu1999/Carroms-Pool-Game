Rules & Mechanics

🟢 Aiming & Striking

Use the modern baseline slider or slide your finger directly on the board baseline to place your striker. Touch and pull back on the striker to aim and set power. Release to strike!

🔴 Classic Mode

Pocket your assigned pieces (White or Black). The Red Queen gives +3 points but must be covered by pocketing one of your own discs immediately on your next shot!

🔵 Disc Pool Mode

No Queen! Just race to clear all your assigned carrom men before your opponent clears theirs.

⚡ Time Attack Blitz

Solo 60-second arcade rush. Each disc pocketed awards +5 seconds (+10s for Queen) and builds a consecutive Combo Score Multiplier!

⚠️ Striker Foul Penalty

If your striker falls into a pocket, it is a foul! You lose your turn and 1 of your previously pocketed pieces is returned to the center of the board.
